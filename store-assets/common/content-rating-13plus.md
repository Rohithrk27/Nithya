# Content Rating Guidance (13+)

Recommended age rating:

- `13+`

## Why 13+

- Habit/game-like progression pressure and challenge mechanics.
- No explicit adult content.
- No real-money gambling.
- No direct user-generated mature media by default.

## Safety Notes for Store Forms

- Violence: none/very mild thematic gamification.
- Sexual content: none.
- Drug/alcohol/tobacco: none.
- Profanity: none by default.
- User interaction: optional community/chat features (if enabled by backend policy).
